'''9995''' is a year in the [[Gaian calendar]], corresponding roughly to Gregorian [[:en:6 BC|6 BC]][[en:6 BC]].

9995 goes from 0006-01-02 BC to 0006-12-31 BC.

This year contains 364 days across the 13 regular months.

9995 corresponds entirely to the Gregorian year [[:en:7 BC|7 BC]][[en:7 BC]].
