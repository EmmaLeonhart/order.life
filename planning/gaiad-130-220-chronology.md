# Gaiad 130–220: Flat Chronology

Browsing surface for the pre-modern human arc. **Not** a chapter plan —
Emma has been explicit that numbered chapter lists in advance of writing
produce garbage. This file gives the events on either end of the gap
and an undifferentiated chronological chart of what happens inside it.
Chapters emerge at the writing desk, not here.
