# Mongolia — Secret History and the Blue Wolf line

Confirmed strong case beyond the numeric tier list (see `structure.md`
§6 "Additional strong cases"). Emma flagged it as needing more
treatment than it has had so far.

## The documentation structure

***The Secret History of the Mongols*** (c. 1240) — compiled shortly
after Genghis's death, in Mongolian script, by people with direct
access to his court and genealogy. An explicit dynastic-origin document
written at the moment the dynasty was at its world-historical peak.

- **Genghis Khan (1162–1227)** — fully historical.
- Traces back through short generational chain to **Börte Chino (Blue
  Wolf)** and **Gua Maral (Fallow Doe)** — the mythic founding pair.
- The mythic-to-historical gap is **unusually short** — a handful of
  generations, not centuries.

## Why it's structurally load-bearing

- **Global gateway-ancestor significance.** Genghis's Y-DNA signature
  is one of the most widespread in the modern world. In a book whose
  genealogical spine is the connective tissue, he has to be a major
  node, not a passing Asian chapter.
- The **Blue Wolf / Fallow Doe** origin is clean mythic material that
  bridges directly into fully-documented history over a short span —
  the rapid-transition hypothesis (see `structure.md` §6) in its purest
  form.
- Emma **wants this treated more fully** than it has been so far —
  flagged explicitly as under-developed.

## Gaiad handling

- At least one chapter for Genghis himself, with downstream-descendant

- Sits in the Asian frame of the book, which is deliberately looser
  than the West Eurasian super-network (see `structure.md` §7), but
  Genghis is one of the Asian nodes that *does* connect massively
  outward — Mongol-Yuan China, Ilkhanid Persia, Golden Horde Russia.
