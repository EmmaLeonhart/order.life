## Key Open Question

**Amniote split (Ch ~63–70 range):** The big gap in the narrative right now is that Tetrus established the tetrapod land nations, but we haven't told the story of how amniotes diverged into:
- **Line A → Sauropsids** (reptiles → dinosaurs → birds)
- **Line B → Synapsids** (pelycosaurs → therapsids → mammals)

This split happened in the Early Carboniferous and needs to be told relatively soon. It's arguably the most important evolutionary story in the upcoming chapters.
